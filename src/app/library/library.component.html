Welcome to the Web School Library

Explore Our Resources

Our library is a hub of knowledge with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources that cover the breadth of computer science and technology. Here, you'll find the perfect environment to research, learn, and expand your understanding of modern computing.

The library is not just about books. It's a space where students come together to share ideas, collaborate on projects, and study in groups. We have designed our space to encourage both individual study and group work.

Getting There

Located at the heart of the campus, the library is accessible from all major buildings within a five-minute walk. Whether you're coming from the dorms, the computer lab, or the lecture halls, just follow the signs for 'Library' posted along the paths.
