Our History

1980s: The Dawn of Computing

Web School was founded at the cusp of the digital revolution. Our first classes were held in basements with bulky computers and floppy disks, igniting a passion for computing in our early students.

1990s: Internet Pioneers

As the internet began to take shape, Web School introduced networking and web development courses, empowering students to create some of the first websites on the World Wide Web.

2000s: The Dot-com Boom

During the dot-com boom, our graduates were at the forefront, joining startups and established tech companies, shaping the internet culture and commerce as we know it today.

2010s and Beyond: Innovators in Technology

Web School continued to evolve with technology, incorporating mobile app development, cybersecurity, and AI into our curriculum, producing innovators and thought leaders in IT.
