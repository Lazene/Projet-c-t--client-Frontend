School Rules and Regulations

General Conduct

At Web School, we foster an environment of respect and integrity. All students and staff are expected to conduct themselves in a manner that reflects the school's values. Disruptive behavior is not tolerated and will be addressed accordingly.

Dress Code

We believe in self-expression while maintaining a professional atmosphere. A smart casual dress code applies during school hours, except for special events where formal attire may be required.

Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is crucial for academic success. Students are required to attend at least 90% of their classes each semester. Exceptions may be made for illness or family emergencies with proper documentation.

Use of Technology

Technology is integral to our curriculum. However, the use of personal devices for non-educational purposes during class is prohibited. This policy ensures that all students remain focused and engaged during lessons.

Academic Integrity

Academic honesty is paramount. Plagiarism, cheating, and other forms of academic dishonesty are strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action, which may include expulsion.
